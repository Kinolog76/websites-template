What are your data protection rights?

boardgamecraft.com wants to ensure that you are fully informed about your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access – You have the right to request copies of your personal data from boardgamecraft.com.

The right to rectification – You have the right to request that boardgamecraft.com correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to ask us to complete any information you consider incomplete.

The right to erasure – You have the right to request that boardgamecraft.com delete your personal data, subject to certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that boardgamecraft.com limit the processing of your personal data under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to boardgamecraft.com processing your personal data under certain conditions.

The right to data portability – You have the right to request that boardgamecraft.com transfer the data we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
